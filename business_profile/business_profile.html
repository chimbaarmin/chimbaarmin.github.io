About "Poshte Boom", Cafe, Tehran, Iran

Poshte Boom Cafe is one of the best Cafe in the center of Tehran. Poshte Boom Cafe gives you a great feeling by bringing your memories to you. The environment of Poshte Boom Cafe is very unique and special which makes this Cafe the only place you like to go.

Old houses used to fill up with strange & familiar people before, but nowadays you can feel that the old houses recall the memories of crowd & busy days in their silence.

Memories of the people who came and left , memories of people who walked, sat, laughed in the yard memories of walnut tree and jasmine bush that people spent days & nights with them in yard. Memory of a man who ran from sunrise to sunset train with trees, kicked and pounded poplar trees just to be fit and ready for any occasion,. The memory of a doleful woman who seemed had nothing to do than sweeps away the dust. From The long room to the small one, from the upper floor to the ground floor, and you could hear every stroke of sweeping the floor every day.

The old house can recall a man who was not there to just walk around in that house and do nothing than live in the moment..
